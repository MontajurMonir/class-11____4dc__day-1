BCB plans to be more selective in issuing NOCs

Word around the Bangladesh Cricket Board (BCB) is that the it will be more selective from now on in giving no objection certificates (NOC) to national team
players and will do a "qualitative assessment" to ensure players don't compete in questionable leagues. NOCs have been a tricky subject in Bangladesh cricket,
with some players regularly getting those from the BCB while others having to pass up lucrative opportunities as the board does now allow them to compete.
